Us

After nearly 1 year studying in Kyoto, Japan together, Peter finally worked up the courage to ask Phoebe out on a date. To wich Phoebe replied, "If I have time....". After a couple of days Phoebe decided that she did indeed have time, so they each put on their best attire and headed off to the Mitarashi Festival at Shimogamo shrine. Now, free of the plague and any misfortune, thanks to the friendly kamisama that resides at the shrine, they plan to spend the rest of their time together.
